Cutting a Polygon Brush

 

Use this tool to cut around figures in the design where the rectangular cut cannot be used because of the proximity of other shapes. This function is similar to the Freehand Cut except that a series of straight lines are used to draw the shape.

To cut a polygon brush, follow these steps:-

  1. Select the button from the toolbox .

  2. Zoom in to the area of your design from which you want to cut your brush.

  3. Select your current background colour . This colour will be transparent when you pick up your brush.

  4. Draw around the shape to be cut using connecting points Â– every time you click with the left-hand mouse button the new point connects with the previous one (rather like dot-to-dot!). You can cut around the shape very accurately using this method.

  5. When you have drawn around the shape, click on the right-hand mouse button. This will connect the start point to the finish point, and the brush will be created.

  6. If you want to make any colours within the brush transparent , do it at this stage.

  7. The brush will now be displayed in the toolbox. Either place it elsewhere in the design or place it into a shelf box .

 

The brush will remain current until you select another brush from the toolbox or until you cut another brush from the design or select one from the shelf.
