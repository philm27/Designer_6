Filling with a Brush

 

It is possible to fill an enclosed area with a custom brush.

To fill with a brush, follow the same procedure as for filling with a single colour Â– just pick up your brush before clicking on the area to fill.

Brush Location

The system uses the current Brush Handle to locate the brush as a repeating pattern in the area to be filled. It will do this even if the shape is the exact shape of the brush or is smaller than it. There is thus always a chance that the edges of the repeating pattern (i.e. the brush) will appear in the filled area.

The brush fills outwards from the point designated by the handle type. For instance, if you have selected a top left handle, the brush will fill across and down from the top left-hand corner of your design. If the brush handle is the centre of the brush then that centre is fixed in the centre of the design and is repeated outwards to fill the design area.

If you are filling an area within your design, rather than the whole area of your design, the brush still fills invisibly from the corners or centre of your design (it does not fill from the corners or centre of the area to be filled).
