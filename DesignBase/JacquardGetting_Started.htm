Getting Started

 

One way to work with the Tutorials is to print them, then open APSO Carpet Designer and complete the short and easy lessons.

 

To print the Quick Tours:

 

Click on the ‘Print’ button. Each topic will be printed out on a separate page.

Alternatively, you can print each Tour out separately. To do this, go to the ‘File’ drop-down menu and choose ‘Print Topic’.
