Quick Outline

This Drawing tool allows you to very quickly outline any individual motif in the design. It is very similar in use to the Flood Fill feature. There are two parts to using Quick Outline:

 

  • You can Right-Click the outline button in the toolbox to display a simple property sheet allowing you to set the Sides and corner style to be outlined
  • Select this as the drawing mode by left clicking the Outline button in the toolbox and then clicking in the design with either the left or right button to outline using the foreground or background color from the palette.

The properties will initially default to the last outline settings used for the current project in  this session.

Simply modify the sides at which the outline will be drawn and/or the corner shape and then click OK.

Unlike the Outline feature on the Design Menu, this does not immediately draw into the design.

Diagonal Lines

These will be outlined providing the appropriate two corners are checked. For example if North and West are selected for outlining, then diagonal lines facing broadly North West will be outlined and will be the correct  thickness measured on the diagonal.

Repeat Match

Outlining will continue across the edge of a repeat and will take into account any drop match or brick repeat.

Drawing a Quick Outline

Simply choose a color from the palette and a line thickness from the list in the tool box. You may also choose a Custom Line width by Right-Clicking  the "Custom" option in the list of line thicknesses.

Then click on any color in the design to outline that motif.

Note that the software will cross  the edges of the repeat and will take the repeat match into account.

In order to protect certain colors from being overwritten you can use the Stencil and/or Protected Areas features.
