Changing Size using Specification

 

Design size can be increased or decreased through the specification dialog. Once you have entered your new design size (in ends and rows or in design measurements) and clicked on OK to accept the alterations, a dimension window will appear on screen. This window allows you to select which edge of your design you want to add to or cut from. For example, if you have increased your design size in the vertical axis, you can choose to add the extra rows all to the top, all to the bottom, or to split them equally adding half to the top and half to the bottom.

 

Designs with Borders

This feature can be used to advantage in order to create the basic layout of a rug or any fabric having a border as follows:

  1. Start a new design and set the size innitially to the size of the centre panel of the rug. Click on OK when the above panel appears. You should now have a design in a solid colour (the current background colour) representing the centre panel of the rug.

  2. Change your background palette colour.

  3. Access the specification window again.

  4. Increase your design size to the full rug size. Click on OK.

  5. In the dimension window make sure that "Add to Both" is selected for width and height. Click on OK. This will add the additional width to both sides equally and similarly for the length. The additional area, the border of the rug, will use the new right hand button colour

This method could in fact be applied several times to build up a series of inner and outer borders of different widths. Just change the background palette colour for every new area you add.

Also, the borders could be added at one side only, if required, by selection of the appropriate option in the above dialog.

You can immediately see your new design size, both in ends/rows and in design measurements, displayed in the status bar .
