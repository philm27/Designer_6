The Sliding Bars

"Click and drag" the sliding button on these controls to the right to increase a value, or to the left to decrease a value.

or, click either side of the sliding button to move it up or down by 16,

or, "Click and hold" on the up/down arrows at the extreme ends of the bars, release the button when the appropriate value is reached,

or, click the up/down arrows one or more times to "nudge" the value up or down as appropriate. Each time the button is clicked
