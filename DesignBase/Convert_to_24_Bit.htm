Convert to 24 Bit

This simple to use option can be found on the Palette Menu.

 

It allows an 8 bit image to be converted to 24 bit from the more normal 8 Bit. To check if the option has worked correctly, hover the cursor over the item on the shelf and it should now say 24 bit in the last few characters after the design name and size,

Example of a design after conversion to 24 bit (cursor hovering over the shelf item)
