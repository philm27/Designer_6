Mitre Design (Diagonal Mirror)

 

The Mitre feature is useful for completing a corner section in a rug by mirroring it at 45 degrees. However it can also be used to mirror a large design section or the whole design.

The quality (or "setting") of the design is taken into account since some redrafting may be required.

The dialog looks like this:

Mitre Dialog as it is initially displayed.

Example of Mitre dialog. In this example, all four corners are to be mitred, the border being 1ft 6in in this case. The directions would be set like this for example when the top and bottom borders of a rug are complete.

 

Procedure

Select the menu option Design->Mitre.

The section of the design that is mitred can be any corner and must be a square section but you can mirror any or all of the four corners as represented by the four buttons and you can specify the size of the square section to be mitred in Ends, Rows, Feet and inches or Metres and Centimetres by choosing from the drop down list.

The four buttons each have 3 states - one for "no mitre" (blank button) and two for the two possible mirror directions at that corner. When the dialog is first displayed, all buttons will be blank.

If the whole design represents a corner section then you should only have one button selected. You should then choose the appropriate corner and direction and set the size to the whole design width or length as appropriate.

Click on OK to apply the mitre. If you are not happy with the results, click on Undo Â– otherwise, click on Close to close the Mitre dialog.
