Brush Orientation

Click on the box to move the dark rectangle around each of its four sides - bottom, top, left, right. The circular border will be formed using the edge indicated by the dark rectangle as the outer edge of the cirlce. (i.e. the brush can represent a portion of bottom, top, left or right hand border).

The images for this option are designed to tie in with the circular border buttons at the top right of the dialog, the oiuter edge being a wider dark grey band than the inner edge.

Brush Orientation

Effect

The bottom edge of the brush will be the outer edge of the border.

The left edge of the brush will be the outer edge of the border.

The top edge of the brush will be the outer edge of the border.

The right edge of the brush will be the outer edge of the border.
