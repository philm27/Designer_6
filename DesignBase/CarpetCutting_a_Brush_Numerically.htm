Cutting a Brush Numerically

 

This facility cuts rectangular or square brushes numerically (i.e. by specifying the co-ordinates at which to cut it). This can be useful if you are cutting a brush from a large design to ensure that it is cut accurately.

Before selecting Numeric Cut you must first determine:

  • The co-ordinates of the bottom left-hand corner of the rectangle to be cut.

  • The size of the brush to be cut in ends and rows (picks).
