Options

The first (left hand) drop down list relates to the horizontal (widthwise) options. The second drop down list relates to the vertical (lengthwise) options.

Options are provided for several common requirements such as placing a brush in the centre of the main design or repeating a motif up the centre of the design or along one edge etc.. In most cases it is not necessary to enter a location nor number of repeats and those boxes will be disabled.
