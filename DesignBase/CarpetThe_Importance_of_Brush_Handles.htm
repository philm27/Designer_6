Brush Handle

 

The brush handle controls the point at which a custom brush is held when placing it in a design. It is an important facility as it will help you to place brushes easily and accurately.

To change the current handle simply select one of the nine possible handles from the menu e.g. Brush->Handle->Centre.

Note that you can also set the default handle for a new brush using preferences. To do this select Edit->Preferences from the menu and then the "Brush" tab.

Note that the Status Bar indicates the current handle location (immediately to the left of the Designe size information). See the topic Status Bar.

You can choose to hold your brush by any of its four corners, at the centre of one edge or by its centre point. The cursors co-ordinates (displayed in the Status Bar) relate to the point at which the brush is held.

Brush handle type also affects placement of brushes when you fill an area with a brush .

When you fill an area with a custom brush, it fills outwards from the point designated by the handle type. For instance, if you had selected a top left handle, the brush would fill out and down from the top left-hand corner of your design. If you are filling an area within your design, rather than the whole area of your design, the brush still fills invisibly from the corners/centre of your design (it does not fill from the corners/centre of the area to be filled).
