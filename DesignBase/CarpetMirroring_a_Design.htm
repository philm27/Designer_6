Mirroring a Design

 

Using this feature you can either mirror one half of the design into the other half, "flip" the whole design, or mirror the whole design and add the mirrored section on to the current design to create a larger design.

Example showing a left to right mirror. Left half of design will be mirrored into right half with no increase in design size. There is no "centre end" i.e. the centre two ends in the design will be identical.

 

Procedure

Select menu option Design->Mirror. This will display the above dialog. You can click on the 6 main option buttons to change the type of mirror.

You should also set the centering option, select "Mirror into doubled up design" if required and click on the Mirror button to complete the operation.

If you make a mistake you can click on Undo and make a change to one or more settings.

Click on Close to close the dialog.

 

Mirror Options

The full list of options is:

  • Left to Right

Mirrors left half of design into right half. Anything already in the right half is overwritten.

  • Right to Left

Mirrors right half of design into left half. Anything already in the left half is overwritten.

  • Horizontal Flip

Flips the whole design left to right

  • Top to Bottom

Mirrors top half of design into the bottom half. Anything already in the bottom half is overwritten.

  • Bottom to Top

Mirrors bottom half of the design into the top half. Anything already in the top half is overwritten.

  • Vertical Flip

Flips the whole design top to bottom

 

 

Centering Option

If the number of Ends (for a left/right mirror) or Rows (for a top/bottom mirror) in the design is an even number then you can choose a centering option. The effect of this on a small design is shown in the following examples.

If there is an odd number of Ends (for Left/Right mirror) or Rows (for top/bottom) mirror then only "Centre End" or "Centre Row" will be available.

 

Mirror into Doubled up Design

If you choose to mirror into a doubled-up design, the design size will first be doubled by adding an area at the appropriate side and the existing design will then be mirrored and placed in the new area.
