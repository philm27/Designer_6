Inserting Text into a Design

 

It is easy to insert text into a design. All the standard fonts available on your computer will be available. The text feature creates a brush containing one or more lines of text which you can then paste into your design and/or place on the shelf in the normal way.

To insert text, click on the text button in the toolbox . The Text Properties dialog will be displayed as shown below.

 

Example of the Text dialog. the user has selected Pristina font and green text, and has typed some text into the text box.

 

Follow these steps to create a piece of text as a brush.

  1. Place your cursor in the Text box and type the text.

  2. Open the drop down list for True Type Font by clicking on the down arrow. You will find a whole range of font types.

  3. Experiment with different font types by selecting them, and seeing their effect on your text in the Preview box.

  4. Experiment with different font sizes by selecting them from the Size drop-down menu, and seeing their effect on your text in the Preview box. The font size in the example is 8. You can also type in a font size if you wish - it does not have to be present in the list, and you can use decimal places e.g. 8.5 etc.

  5. If the preview is not big enough to view the whole text, you can re-size the dialog by dragging any of the four sides. (See example below)

  6. When you are happy with your font type and size, choose a justification (left, centre or right). If you have more than one row of text it will align to your chosen justification. In the example, the text is aligned to the left.

  7. Your design color palette will be found in the Palette drop-down menu select your text color from this palette.

  8. When you are happy with your text, click OK to exit the Text Properties window. Your text will now be held as a custom brush either place it in your design or drop it into a shelf.

Re-Sizing the Dialog

Example of the Text Dialog after being re-sized by dragging the bottom right corner. You can drag any of the 4 sides or 4 corners. Also, the user has modified the size to 24 and the justification to centre.

To re-seize the dialog drag any of the 4 sides or 4 corners using the mouse.

Background Color

The background color will be set the current right hand button color but note that the brush will initially have a layer mask masking off the background. You will see this color if you place the brush on the shelf and select Delete Layer mask from the Shelf pop-up menu.
