Thumbnails

Design Thumbnails are shown in this area. They may all be zoomed in or out simultaneously using the zoom tool at the bottom right and the user can switch between the "SIM" preview and the "Artwork" preview.

A search may result in several pages of thumbnails and the user can move between the pages using the forward and backward arrows at the middle bottom of the display.

To select a design for loading click on it using the left hand mouse button. To load it click on the "Leaf" motif at the bottom right.
