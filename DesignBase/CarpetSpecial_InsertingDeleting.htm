Special Inserting/Deleting

 

This feature lets you insert or delete (remove) a full width or full length section in the design without affecting motifs within the design. It allows you to choose the path across your design, in which to insert or delete ends or rows (picks), which will cause the least damage to your artwork.

Procedure

Follow these steps to insert or delete special ends/rows:-

  1. Select menu option Design->Insert Many or Design->Delete as appropriate.

  2. From the sub-menu, select Ends Special or Rows Special as appropriate.

  3. Create a line from one side of the design to its opposite edge by clicking with the mouse at connecting points. Each time you click, your line will join from the previous connecting point to the new one. If you dont start/finish at an edge, the computer will join your first/last connecting point to the edge of the design.

  1. Having reached the opposite edge with your line, click the right-hand mouse button. One of the four dialogs "Insert Ends" "Delete Ends" "Insert Rows" or "Delete Rows" will be displayed (see below).

  2. Enter the number of ends or rows to be inserted or deleted and click on OK.

  3. Your ends/rows will be inserted or deleted. If you are inserting ends/rows, they will be inserted in your current background colour .

Similar dialogs to the above will be displayed for Delete Special Rows and for Insert Special Rows.

Note that you cannot insert special lines across the edges of a repeating design, so it is advisable to work with the grid on when using this application, so that you can see the edges for start and finish points.
