Pressure Controls Line Thickness

This applies when you work in Straight Line Draw or Circle , Ellipse , Square and Rectangle Draw  where it applies to the shape outline. If you tick this option the line will get thicker or thinner when you apply more or less pressure with the pen. The line will not gradate as it does with Freehand Draw  the whole shape outline will remain of constant thickness.

To deactivate this facility, click on the tick to remove it from the box.
