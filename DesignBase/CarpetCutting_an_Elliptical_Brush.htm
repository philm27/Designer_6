Cutting an Elliptical Brush

 

To cut an elliptical brush, follow these steps:-

 

  1. Select the button from the toolbox , or alternatively use the keyboard shortcut CTRL and E.

  2. Zoom in to the area of your design from which you want to cut your brush.

  3. Select your current background colour . This colour will be transparent when you pick up your brush.

  4. Click & drag to create an ellipse enclosing the motif.

  5. If you want to make any colours within the brush transparent , do it at this stage.

  6. The brush will now be displayed in the toolbox. Either place it elsewhere in the design or place it into a shelf box .

 

The brush will remain current until you select another brush from the toolbox or until you cut another brush from the design or select one from the shelf.
