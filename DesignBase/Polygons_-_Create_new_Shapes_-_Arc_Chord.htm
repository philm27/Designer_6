Polygons - Create new Shapes - Arc/Chord

This is the Arc/Chord tab on the Create New Shape Property Sheet. Use it to control the "from" and "to" angle for an arc or chord. This tab applies only to circle and ellipse shapes in which Arc or Chord has been selected.

For more general information see the topic Polygons - Standard Shapes.

 

An example of the Arc/Chord tab showing a figure based on a circle having a radios of 2 feet with a start angle of zero and a finish angle of 90 degrees. THe figure in this case has been split into 5 segments by the radial lines.

Start Angle and Finish Angle

You can modify the Start or Finish Angle by one of the following methods:

  • Click in the "clock" face to cause the rotation angle to change by 5 degrees towards the cursor

  • Click on and drag the pointer round the "clock" face

  • Click in the Text Edit box and type a new value.
