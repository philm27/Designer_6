The Grid

 

It is possible to place a grid over your design Â– to separate each end row(pick), with bold lines at intervals set in the Specification Dialog.

Also, if the grid is on, the edge of the design repeat will be shown in red and the centre of the repeat will be outlined in red. The centre will normally be four points (as shown in the right hand example below) for designs having an even width and length in ends and rows(picks). However if the design has an odd width then the one centre end will be outlined and similarly if the length is odd then the one central row(pick) will be outlined. Therefore if both width and length are odd then only a single central point will be outlined.

Example of a large design showing black grid lines and bold lines and the edges of the repeat in red. This example shows the point where the corners of a straight match meet.

Another example showing the 4 grid points at the centre of the repeat outlined in red.

 

You can turn the grid on or off in three ways:-

  • Click on the button in the toolbox.

  • Click on the button in the toolbar.

  • Use the keyboard shortcut <G>.

 

If you are working at a very low zoom level the grid may only show the red lines marking the design repeat, at higher zoom levels, the bold lines will be shown and at even higher zoom levels the full grid will be shown.
