Duplicate Full Width or Full Length

 

These options duplicate the whole design, placing additional repeats to the right of the original (duplicate full width) or "above" the original (duplicate full length).

Select menu options Design->Duplicate->Full Width or Design->Duplicate->Full Length as appropriate.

Example Duplicate Full Width dialog

In both cases a dialog will appear so that the number of additional repeats can be specified.

 

Repeat Match

Note that the repeat match will be taken into account and the drop or shift in the specification will be amended. So, for example, if a design has a quarter drop and you double its width from, say, 12 inches to 24 inches, a second repeat will be added to the right of the first, dropped by 3 inches (i.e. a quarter drop) and the whole thing will then be a half drop design.
