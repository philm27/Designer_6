Tutorials Introduction

 

The Tutorials allow you to become familiar with some of the commonly used software features by actually creating a design and saving it so that you are then in a position to create designs of your own.

To become proficient in the use of the APSO software will obviously take a lot of practice but the tutorials should start you off in the right direction so that you can then use the help files as and when necessary to fill in the gaps in your knowledge.

There are four Tutorials. 1 and 2 are textile oriented. 3 and 4 are carpet oriented though they will all be of general interest to designers as they all use a variety of techniques.

Tutorial 1 is a basic introduction to creating and saving artwork.

Tutorial 2 is a more complex design and again takes you through to the artwork stage.

Tutorial 3 creates a medallion design in a repeating field and a variation of it.

Tutorial 4 creates a rug by using and developing the field design created in the preceding tutorial.

 

Tutorial 3 takes the design created in Tutorial 2 and applies weaves to it.
