Print Preferences

 

You can find these options by choosing Edit > Preferences from the menu bar.

 

 

Here you can:

 

  • Select the background colour for the printed page - simply click on the colour that you wish to apply to the page.

  • Choose the order in which to print your pages - Left to Right or from Top to Bottom.

  • Set variable levels for the colour and frame numbers - set the numbers to occur however often you want in the columns and rows of the print-out.

 

Click OK once you are finished.
