Logo Location

If you want your logo to be placed in a corner or on an edge of the layout, select your required position by clicking in one of the eight circles within this square.

If you want your logo to be placed in a pre-determined position, you must first note the co-ordinates required for the top left-hand corner of the logo (you will see these in the status bar when you float your cursor around the layout). By clicking in the central circle within this square, you will then be able to enter your co-ordinates in the boxes on the right.
