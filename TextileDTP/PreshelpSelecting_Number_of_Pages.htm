Selecting Number of Pages

 

Having selected your printer and paper size , you need to input the number of horizontal and vertical pages that you require for your layout. For instance, if you want to print a large design at full scale, you are likely to want to lay it out over several pages.

To input number of pages, select PAGE SETUP from the FILE drop-down menu Â– the following window will appear on the screen. Click on the properties within it to find out more:-

 

 

When you have selected a page setup, you then have the option to

 

  • Show the page outlines on your layout

  • Show the page margins

  • Clip your objects so that they stay within the page margins

 

Page Setup can be altered at any time. If you decide that you need more or less pages as you design your layout, simply go to PAGE SETUP and enter your new values.

There are times when you will be prompted by the computer to alter your page setup. Examples are:-

 

  • if you increase the scale of a design within your layout, so that it is too large to fit on the page(s).

  • if you try to insert a frame plan (Carpet only) that wil not fit on the page(s).
