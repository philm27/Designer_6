Cropping Designs

 

Using the Mouse

To crop the design in order to display a specific area of it, follow these steps:-

 

  1. Select the design to be cropped  the handles will appear at the corners and edges of the design.

  2. Select the icon from the toolbox .

  3. Float the cursor over a handle until the cursor turns into:-

 

  • A double-headed horizontal arrow (Vertical edge handles)- Click and drag towards the centre of the design to crop it in the width.

  • A double-headed vertical arrow (Horizontal edge handles)- Click and drag towards the centre of the design to crop it in the length.

  • A double-headed diagonal arrow (Corner handles)- Click and drag towards the centre of the design to crop it in both the width and length.

 

Using the Object Properties

Designs can also be cropped numerically through the Current Design Properties window:-

 

  1. Double click on the design to be cropped to open the window.

  2. Click on the Size tab to bring it to the foreground.

 

  1.  

  1. Input the co-ordinates for the bottom left-hand corner of the section of design required  in the Start End and Start Row override boxes.

  1. Input the size of section required in the horizontal (Ends) and vertical (Rows).

  2. Click on OK to close the window. The design will be automatically cropped to the size specified.
