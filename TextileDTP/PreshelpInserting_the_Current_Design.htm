Inserting the 'Current' Design

 

To insert the current design into the working area :-

 

  1. Click on the icon in the toolbox , or alternatively select DESIGN from the INSERT drop-down menu or the pop-up menu (right click in the design).

  2. Click and drag using the left-hand mouse button to create a rectangle within the working area. The size of the rectangle will determine the scale at which your design will be inserted.

  3. When you release the mouse button, the design will be inserted.
