Scale

If you select Scale, you must then input your own scale in the box on the right. (It is greyed out in this example as Fit to Page has been selected.)

Enter the required scale either by:-

  • Clicking on the arrow buttons to the right of the number box, or

  • Highlighting the current number (by dragging the cursor over it whilst holding down the left-hand mouse button) and then typing in the new value with the keyboard.

The scale you choose will not necessarily fit into your Page Setup (in the Advanced tab). If you set your own scale, you must calculate the number of pages required and enter them in the Advanced tab.
