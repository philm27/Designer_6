Selecting a Printer

 

Your currently selected printer will be displayed on the status bar. If you need to print to a different printer, select PRINTER SETUP from the FILE drop-down menu Â– this will open the Print Setup window.

Select your required printer from the drop-down menu (accessed by the button) and then click on OK to accept the change.

 

If the printer you require is not listed in the drop-down menu, you need to add the printer. To add a printer, follow these steps:-

 

  1. Click with the left-hand mouse button on the START button on the task bar

  2. Click on SETTINGS to reveal a sub-menu

  3. Click on PRINTERS to open the PRINTERS window.
