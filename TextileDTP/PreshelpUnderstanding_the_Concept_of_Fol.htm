Understanding the Concept of Folders

 

Designs, palettes and layouts are all saved as FILES within FOLDERS. They are differentiated by file extensions (i.e. palettes have a file extension of .PAL, layouts have a file extension of .LYT).

The easiest way to think of your folders is as a family tree. Here is a typical example of a folder layout within the computers hard disk:-

 

 

The example only follows one branch of the tree at each junction. Obviously, there would be similar branches out of CONTRACT as there are out of DOMESTIC and so on.

 

Use folders to keep your work ordered and easy to find. The above example is only very simple  you can create as many folders as you like along each generation of the family tree to suit your specific requirements.
