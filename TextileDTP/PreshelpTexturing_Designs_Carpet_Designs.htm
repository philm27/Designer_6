Texturing Designs (Carpet Designs only)

 

It is possible to apply texture to a design in two ways  either to texture a design that is already in the layout, or to import a design with texture applied.

To texture a design that is already in the layout:-

 

  1. Double-click on the design to access the Current Design Properties window.

  2. Click on the Texture tab to bring it to the foreground.

 

Here is the tab. Click on the properties within it to find out more:-

 

 

  1. The bitmap files of cut and loop are 3 inches square. Therefore you must make sure that the bitmap is a multiple of your design repeat.

  2. Example: design width = 108/3=36.

  3. To set this correctly make sure you have selected "Lock to Size" and then you can type in the size of texture that you require to cover your design repeat.

  4. The threshold value will allow you to darken or lighten the whole of your texture overall.

To import a design with texture applied:-

 

  1. Select TEXTURE from the INSERT drop-down menu  the Open window will be displayed on screen.

  2. Navigate through the folders and select your design file.

  3. Click and drag using the left-hand mouse button to create a rectangle within the working area. The size of the rectangle will determine the scale at which your design will be inserted.

  4. When you release the mouse button, the design will be inserted with texture applied.

  5. To alter the texture settings, double-click on the design and make adjustments in the Texture tab.
