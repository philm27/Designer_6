Selecting Paper Size

 

Before starting to create your layout, make sure that the paper size is correct  as this will affect the layout size. Look at the status bar to see the current paper size.

To change paper size, select PRINTER SETUP from the FILE drop-down menu  this will open the Print Setup window. The bottom half of the window deals specifically with paper:-

 

 

Select your paper size and paper source (i.e. manual feed tray, automatically select) by using the drop-down menus  accessed by clicking on the buttons.

Also choose your paper orientation here  select Portrait or Landscape as appropriate.

Click on OK to accept the changes.

Although you can change paper size and orientation as often as you wish, it is useful to set up printer defaults for your most commonly used specifications.
