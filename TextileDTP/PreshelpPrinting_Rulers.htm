Printing Rulers

 

It is possible to add "Rulers" along any or all of the four edges of a design. The ruler can show end/row/pick numbers or measurements in inches/feet/yards/cm/Mtrs etc.

To control Rulers use the Object Properties which are accessed by double-clicking on the design. Click on the Rulers tab to bring it to the foreground.

For more information see the topic Design Rulers Properties.
