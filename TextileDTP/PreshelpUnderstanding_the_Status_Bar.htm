Understanding the Status Bar

 

The status bar runs along the bottom of the screen. It enables you to view, at a glance, your current set-up. Here is a sample status bar (it has been split into two sections in this instance):-

 

 

Reading from left to right, you are given the following information:

 

  • You are currently working in Select , Move and Resize mode.

  • You have selected the HPDeskJet 340 printer , printing from Local Port (LPT) 1 on A4 sized paper .

  • Your current default scale is 1:3

  • The time is 11.21am

  • Your cursor is at the co-ordinates 79.4 (in the horizontal axis), 176.3 (in the vertical axis).
