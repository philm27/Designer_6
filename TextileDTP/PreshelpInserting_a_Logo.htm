Inserting a Logo

 

If you have a company logo saved as a file, you can insert it into your layout. You can insert it at any scale, so donÂ’t worry about its size in relation to your layout.

You can also use the same technique to insert a picture or photograph.

To insert a logo, follow these steps:-

  1. Right click in the layout to display the pop-up menu and select menu item Insert-> Logo. This will bring the Open window on screen so that you can navigate to the required folder and select your logo.

  2. Click on Open to load the logo and close the window.

  3. Float the cursor over the working area to position your logo.

  4. Click and drag using the left-hand mouse button to create a rectangle. The size of the rectangle will determine the scale at which your logo will be inserted.

  5. When you release the mouse button, the logo will be inserted.

  6. You can now move and scale the logo as required.

As an alternative to step 1 you can click on the icon in the toolbox, or, main menu item Insert->Logo.

There is also a related menu item Insert->Quick Insert->Logo. If you have a logo selected in the Template Setup window, you can select this function to quickly place the logo into your layout. It will be placed in the position selected in Template Setup.
