Understanding Working Boundaries

 

Unlike APSO's Textile Design packages (Jacquard, Carpet, Dobby and Textile Designer), with Textile DTP the whole of the screen is used as a working area. You can place objects anywhere between the bottom of the toolbar and the top of the status bar they do not have to be on the page. However, when you print your work, only objects that lie within the margins on the page will printed.

This feature can be particularly useful if you:-

 

  • Want to print information associated with a design  for instance a palette or frame plan (Carpet only)  without printing the design. Simply position the design on either side of the page(s) so that it does not print with your layout.
