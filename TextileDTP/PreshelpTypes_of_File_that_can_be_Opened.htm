File Types that can be Opened

 

You will find all the filetypes that can be opened in Textile DTP in the "Files of Type" drop-down menu within the Open File window when you import a design .

They are:-

IFF & .PRJ, .BMP, .JPG, .TIF, .PAT, .EP, .WEA, .CPT, .DES, .TSF, VCF, KNF & .PCX

Usually, you will only use .IFF and .PRJ files to open designs and projects previously saved in Textile Designer. If you have a scanner, then you will also use .bmp files.

As well as importing designs that have previously been saved in Textile Designer, you may also wish to import scans or images from 3Di, to include in your presentation.

The other file types are available to you in case you need to import files into Textile Designer from other sources.
