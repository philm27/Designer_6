Expanding Designs in Repeat

 

To display a design in multiple repeats:-

 

  1. Select the design to be expanded  the handles will appear at the corners and edges of the design.

  2. Select the icon from the toolbox .

  3. Float the cursor over a handle until the cursor turns into:-

 

  • A double-headed horizontal arrow (Vertical edge handles)- Click and drag away from the design to expand it in the width.

  • A double-headed vertical arrow (Horizontal edge handles)- Click and drag away from the design to expand it in the length.

  • A double-headed diagonal arrow (Corner handles)- Click and drag away from the design to expand it in both the width and length.

 

Designs can also be expanded into repeat numerically, in the Current Design Properties window:-

 

  1. Double-click on the design to access the window.

  2. Click on the Repeats tab to bring it to the foreground.

 

  1.  

  1. Input the number of horizontal and vertical repeats required  partial repeats can be displayed if required.

  2. Click on OK to close the window and return to the layout. The design will now be displayed in repeat.
