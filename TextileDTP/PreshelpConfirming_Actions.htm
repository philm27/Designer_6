Confirming Actions

 

As a safety measure, APSO recommend that you always select CONFIRMATION from the VIEW drop-down menu  a shows that it is selected.

By selecting Confirmation, every time you perform a critical task (such as deleting an object from the layout), you will be asked to confirm your action. This gives you the opportunity to cancel the action if required.

If you do not use Confirmation whilst working on the layout, you may make an unwanted alteration to the layout  which you will not be able to undo.
