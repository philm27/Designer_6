Adjusting the Color Palette

 

It is possible to adjust the RGB values of palette colors prior to printing or to import shades previously saved in your Yarns Database.

It is also possible to make colors within a design transparent. This option is useful for complex presentations - you may want to make a design color transparent so that a design can be seen beneath it.

Color palette adjustments are made in the Design Properties window. Double-click on the design and then click on the Color tab to bring it to the foreground. For more information on how to use this tab see the topic Design Color Properties.
