Aligning Objects

 

You can align selected objects together, to create a neat, professional layout.

To align objects:-

  1. Decide which object you wish to align to. This object will remain stationary whilst the other selected objects move to align to it.

  2. Click on the icon in the toolbox.

  3. Hold down the SHIFT key on the keyboard and, keeping the key held down, click once on each object that you wish to align  make sure that you click on the object you wish to align to last.

  4. Release the SHIFT key. All of the objects that you have selected will have for handles , except for the object that is being aligned to  which will have for handles.

Whilst the objects remain selected, you can align them in different ways as often as you wish. You can also space them evenly whilst they remain selected as a group.

If you want to move the objects, they will move as a group whilst they remain selected.

Deselect the objects by clicking once outside the group  the handles will disappear.
