Spacing Objects

Selected objects can be spaced evenly within the layout. To achieve this:-

 

  1. Select the icon from the toolbox.

  2. Click and drag a rectangle to encompass all of the objects that you want to space evenly.

    OR

    Click once on each object that you want to space evenly whilst holding down the SHIFT key.

  3. Click on one of the spacing tools on the toolbar :-

 

    1. Spaces all of the objects evenly vertically.

      Spaces all of the objects evenly horizontally.

      Spaces all of the objects evenly in both directions.
