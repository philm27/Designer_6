Weave Alignment Preference

 

This feature is particularly appropriate for Wilton Carpet and Pile Fabric designers. It should generally NOT be used if your are designing Flat Cloth fabrics.

The Alignment option in Jacquard Preferences allows weaves which are one dent wide to be aligned with the motif in the artwork making the design of products using weaves longer than 1 design row very easy.

The image on the left is a section of an original design (NTTB). The background is to be single loops of frame 1. The large diamonds are to be double loops of frame 2 and the small diamonds are to be double loops of frame 3.

The image shown on the right is a section of the design after weaves have been applied.

The double loop weaves are 4 picks long and 1 dent (3 frames) wide and, because of the above preference (Align Single Dent weaves with motif), each sequence of loops has been aligned with the start of the motif in that dent.

This preference therefore avoids having to use two different colours, one for the start of a loop and one for the continuation of a loop.

Without the Alignment preference, the weave would look like the image below. All weaves are aligned with end 1 pick1 so all the double loops are in a line and single loops occur at the top and bottom of alternate dents in the diamond motif.
